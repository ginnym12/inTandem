IN TANDEM WITH CHANGE



Two men on bicycles, searching for connection through music.

M I S S I O N




We (Jason and Eric) are embarking upon a bicycle trip this summer (June - August) from Raleigh, NC to New Orleans, LA, and we need your help! This rolling musical love machine seeks to document the human experience through the powers of music, motion, and storytelling. We will be filming a documentary and actively producing a collection of music over the course of the summer as we work our way through the deep South. We believe that film and music presents a relatable perspective which enables an audience to feel something deeply and think intently, in a way that can promote positive change within our community. We hope to utilize music as a universal language in order to foster real connections between ourselves and the people we meet along our travels.

S T O R Y



This idea was essentially born in New Orleans, during a trip that we took last Winter. Our experiences busking on the NOLA streets connected us with so many kind, insightful, and talented individuals; most of whom were living on the city streets and playing music to sustain themselves. We began to realize and understand the possibility for connection and discovery within this population of people who are mislabeled and devalued by our societal structure. We decided that pursuing these connections and tapping into this underrepresented world of street musicians was more than a worthwhile endeavor.

We will be traveling with a bike trailer full of instruments, as well as all of the recording equipment necessary to record and produce the music we will be hearing and playing. We intend to record and produce street musicians who may not possess the connections or financial means to produce their own music. We then plan to release the music on this website.

On a less serious note, we also believe that the shenanigans we are bound to experience over the course of two months traveling 1,562 miles through the heat of the Southern Summer on a bicycles will be worth keeping up with. Thank you so much for your support. We all stand in tandem with change.

S U P P O R T



Beyond the change invoked in our souls and those we encounter on our journey, we plan on producing two tangible "products" from this trip:

  1. FILM: This will be an encompassing documentary of our entire trip. It will include our story, as well as the story of those we encounter along the way. This we be our vehicle for sharing our perspective on our experiences in the most organic way possible.
  2. ALBUM: We will produce a musical production which comprises a compilation of songs written/played/recorded by us and those we meet on our travels. We will distribute / sell this album, and donate all of the proceeds to those who helped create the album.
