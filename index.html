IN TANDEM WITH CHANGE



Two men, on a tandem bicycle, journeying across the United States in search of music, compassion, and insight.

M I S S I O N




This rolling musical love machine seeks to catalyze the public sphere through the powers of music, motion, and storytelling. We feel a fervent conviction in the power which music holds, and its ability to foster raw, intimate, infinite, sentiments within human beings. This project serves as a grassroots initiative intended to break barriers which prevent and deter us from fostering genuine connections with peoples of all walks of life. We hope that this project may stand as an example of the potential which exists within us: to pursue genuine connections with every and all humans through whatever vehicle they see fit. For us specifically, making music is that vehicle.

S T O R Y



We are embarking upon a journey across the North American continent in search of people; people to meet, to talk to, to share with, to exist with. Above all else, we seek Connection. We seek to foster connections specifically with peoples who are marginalized, underserved, and stratified by a system which instills a very biased, rigid, convoluted idea of what it means to be a person of worth. We hope to use music - playing and sharing music - as a means of emitting and expressing energies of loving kindness that will serve to establish connections between us and our listeners. In addition to playing music for people, we seek to meet other people who wish to express themselves through the vehicle of music, and collaborate in creating and playing original musical compositions. We will be traveling with a mobile, minimalist recording studio, in order to record the music that we hope to make with the people we meet.

Upon the conclusion of our trip, we hope to construct a consolidated work of the music shared by those who we meet on our travels, as well as any music we were inspired to create through the journey. This work will be produced and distributed publicly, with the intention of generating support for those who took a part in its creation, through both monetary contributions, and the establishment of a listener base for these aspiring artists. We will be filming the trip in hopes of ascertaining some final production of videography which illustrates the lives of those we meet and have the opportunity to form a relationship with - their stories, struggles, sentiments, hopes, dreams - which we hope will provide the viewer with a deepened understanding for his or her own privileges, in an effort to generate some sense of love, compassion, and empathy with the world, whether it be for one moment, or a lifetime.

S U P P O R T



Beyond the change invoked in our souls and those we encounter on our journey, we plan on producing two tangible "products" from this trip:

  1. FILM: We will produce a film which details our experiences of the trip, the people we meet on the trip, and displays these people and our experiences in an organic way which illustrates our mutual connections as people existing in the same time and space.
  2. ALBUM: We will produce a musical production which comprises a compilation of songs written/played/recorded by us and those we meet on our travels. We will distribute / sell this album, and donate all of the proceeds to those who helped create the album.
